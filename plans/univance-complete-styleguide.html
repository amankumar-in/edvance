Univance Style Guide

This style guide defines the visual language for the Univance educational platform. It provides guidelines for colors, typography, and design principles to ensure consistency across all interfaces.

Design Principles

  • No shadows - Use background colors instead of shadows for depth and separation
  • Flat design - Clean, flat surfaces without 3D effects
  • Slightly rounded corners - Use border-radius for friendly aesthetic
  • Structured grid - Consistent alignment and spacing
  • Purposeful color - Colors have specific meanings and functions
  • Typography hierarchy - Clear distinction between heading, body, and display text
  • White space - Generous use of white space for clarity
  • Minimal decoration - Visual elements serve a purpose
  • Use Images - Use pictures and vector artowrk more than icons
