Brand Guidelines & Design System

A comprehensive visual language that brings our educational platform to life with Swiss design principles: clarity, minimalism, and purpose.

Brand Story

Univance combines "Universal" and "Advance," reflecting our mission to provide universal access to advanced educational tools that help every student progress.

Our Mission

Univance is an educational platform designed to transform the way students, teachers, and parents engage with learning through motivation, tracking, and celebration of academic progress.

We believe that education thrives when students are actively engaged, properly motivated, and have the right tools to track their progress.

Our Values

  • Accessibility – Education for everyone
  • Engagement – Learning through active participation
  • Progress – Celebrating every achievement
  • Community – Growing together

Design Philosophy

Our design approach embraces Swiss Design principles: minimalism, clarity, and objective organization with strong typography and purposeful use of color.

We employ a flat design aesthetic that prioritizes usability and readability while creating clear hierarchical structures for different user groups.
